Gireum Tteokbokki

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs cylindrical tteok
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp plus 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp coarse gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp fine gochugaru
  • 1 tbsp light corn syrup
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil over medium-high heat. Meanwhile, prepare the marinade.
  2. Stir together the soy sauce, sugar, coarse and fine gochugaru, corn syrup, garlic and sesame oil in a large bowl and set aside.
  3. Add the tteok to the boiling water and cook, stirring constantly to ensure nothing sticks to the bottom of the pot, until softened, about 2 minutes. Drain, toss, and coat the tteok completely in the marinade. If any tteok stick together, separate them and toss well.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of the neutral oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add the gochugaru tteok and its marinade and repeat the cooking process, until the tteok is slightly dark red and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Gochugaru can burn easily so it is important to keep stirring and tossing.
